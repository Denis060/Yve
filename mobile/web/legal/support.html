Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?

Just open Yve — no account is required to begin. Tap Scan to photograph an assignment, or start a chat and ask Yve a question in plain language. Yve works right away on the free plan.

How do I sign in or save my work across devices?

Open the Profile tab and tap "Sign in to keep your work." Enter your email and we'll send you a 6-digit code to type back into the app — no password to remember. Signing in keeps your subjects, chats, and progress synced across devices.

What does the free plan include?

The free plan lets you scan, chat, organize subjects, upload materials, use voice, and review what you've learned. Some daily usage limits apply.

How do I cancel a subscription?

On iPhone and iPad, manage or cancel any subscription in Settings → your name (Apple Account) → Subscriptions. On the web or Android, manage it from the Profile tab in Yve.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open Profile and tap Delete account, or see our account deletion page. We permanently remove your data within 30 days.

The microphone, camera, or notifications aren't working.

Yve asks for these permissions only when you use the related feature. If you declined earlier, you can re-enable them in your device's Settings → Yve. Then reopen the feature in the app.

Is my data private?

Yes. We never sell your data and don't use it for advertising. Read the full Privacy Policy for details on what we collect and why.
